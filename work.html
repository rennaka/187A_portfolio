Future Project.

More information on Walmart Labs

One of my future projects within the Cognitive Science Department will be conducting ethnographic research. My primary goal is to design a more efficient process when customers interact with POS (Point Of Sale) systems. My first objective is to develop a prototype utilizing a new technology in order to drastically increase any retailers transactions per minute.

Boltman.

Don't forget to Like Boltman on Facebook!

"Social media" is something that has gained my interest recently with the emergence of websites like Facebook, Twitter or Myspace. Through the random cards that life deals, I was presented the opportunity to interact with fans as a page administrator.
